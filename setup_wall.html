Setup a card wall EWall likes

Materials recommendation:

  • quad ruled, ivory paper card
  • black color wall with light color cards
  • white color wall with deep color cards

Setup recommendation:

  • Use card to define column head ("Ready for Dev", "Dev in Progress", "Done", etc.). See black & white wall examples. EWall assumes first row cards are column heads.
  • Ideally, cards should be the only thing on the wall. EWall can detect cards with team member pictures or notes near/on the card. But the more of these non-cards info on the wall, the more likely EWall will mis-detect the cards need to be tracked.
  • There should be more than 10 cards on the wall, including column heads.
  • Leave some room between columns, so that EWall can group cards into columns.

Photograph recommendation:

  • 8-megapixel camera is good for a 3 meters width card wall in our test, for example: iPhone 4S.
  • A clear picture is more important than high resolution picture, so something like camera tripod fixing the camera can help a lot.
